The Challenge & Our Solution

The Problem

East Africa's history is rich, but much of it risks being forgotten or lost. Key historical figures and cultural narratives remain undocumented, under-documented, or inaccessible to the wider public.

  • Without systematic documentation, many contributions to East Africa's identity and heritage will fade.
  • Independent filmmakers face financial challenges in producing long-form documentaries with historical depth.
  • Existing archives are often donor-funded and not easily accessible to the public.
