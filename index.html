Our Mission

Preserving East Africa's rich history through innovative digital storytelling and documentary filmmaking.

The Challenge

East Africa's history is rich, but much of it risks being forgotten or lost. Key historical figures and cultural narratives remain undocumented, under-documented, or inaccessible to the wider public.

  • Without systematic documentation, many contributions to East Africa's identity and heritage will fade.
  • Independent filmmakers face financial challenges in producing long-form documentaries with historical depth.
  • Existing archives are often donor-funded and not easily accessible to the public.

Our Solution

We've created an innovative platform where history meets modern storytelling:

  • Short-form (5‚Äì15 mins) and long-form (30+ mins) documentaries are professionally produced and curated
  • Accessible content through modern digital platforms
  • Proceeds fund ongoing historical documentation projects
  • Collaborative approach with local communities and historians

Our Vision

To become the premier digital archive of East African historical narratives, making our shared heritage accessible to generations to come.

  • Build a sustainable platform for historical preservation
  • Create educational resources for schools and institutions
  • Foster cross-generational dialogue about our history
  • Empower local storytellers and filmmakers
